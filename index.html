About Me

I am a San Francisco native, currently residing in Oakland. I am a web developer specializing in Ruby on Rails and Backbone.js/JavaScript, and am always expanding my repertoire to new languages and technologies. Web development encompasses everything I really enjoy: creating, designing, building, and problem-solving.

I discovered my passion for coding in high school, when I taught myself HTML and CSS and started building websites as a hobby. I studied linguistics in college, and I bring this strong analytical background into my passion for programming. I love the combined challenge of creativity and problem solving that comes with coding, and the satisfaction that comes with finding an elegant solution to a problem.

My languages and technologies include: Ruby, Rails, SQL, JavaScript, Backbone.js, HTML, CSS, SASS, jQuery, jQuery UI, TDD (RSpec & Capybara)
